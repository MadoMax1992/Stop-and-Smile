Lessons

Coverage

Single has the ball, and is the On-Official.
Single and Lead need to communicate “ON” and “OFF” the ball.

Lead has the front of the crease, and is the Off-Official.

Trail is helping on crease, and watching the shooter.

Positioning

Single is about 5 yards upfield and 7-10 yards out from the goal.
Must move in if Lead gets pushed off the goal-line.

Lead is close to crease, and keeps play in front of him. Moves in and out with ball.

Trail, after checking with CBO, jogs to about 5 yards downfield, and 5 yards in from the end of restraining line Covers side line.

Penalty Realay

Single calls out “Flag down”, stops play when appropriate. Gets out to an open area, and gives signal.
Single signals to Trail.

Lead, who is not involved in reporting penalty, be sure to get the ball ready for play!

Trail relays to Table. The Bench Manager then verifies with the Table.

Fight Procedure

Get numbers. Blow whistle very loudly and use a long blast. Be careful handling players.

Trail must freeze the benches in his back and help control game.

CBO must freeze the far-side benches and help control game.
